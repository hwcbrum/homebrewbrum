What is Homebrew Website Club?

Homebrew Website Club meetups take place around the world. It’s a meetup of creatives passionate about improving their own websites, sharing successes & challenges with a like-minded and supportive community.

Much of the discussion is centered around the IndieWeb, which is about owning our own online identities and data. Each month we’ll pick a different theme to focus on before opening up the conversation.

When and where?

Birmingham’s Homebrew Website Club meets monthly on the last Wednesday of each month from 6:00pm until 9:00pm.

This meetup is intentionally informal with no talks. We kick off at 6pm where you’ll get to hack on your website or project. From 7pm we’ll introduce ourselves and share what we’ve been working on. Each meetup has a theme, which we’ll discuss at 7:30pm. And then from 8:00pm, we’ll open up the discussion to whatever anyone wants to talk about.

There's free wifi and you'll have the opportunity to grab a drink and snack.

We keep a little mailing list to let people know when the next meetup is. Feel free to add your email and we’ll pop you an email or two each month with information.

Of course, we won't share or spam your email
