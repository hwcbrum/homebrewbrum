Homebrew Website Club Birmingham

Are you building your own website? Creating an open source project? Writing a blog? Or some other digital venture?

Join like-minded people each month in Birmingham to exchange ideas, receive feedback, talk shop, help on projects or just for some good conversation and coffee.

What is Homebrew Website Club?

Homebrew Website Club Birmingham is a monthly meetup of people passionate about creating, improving, designing and building their own website, in the same structure as the classic Homebrew Computer Club meetings.

When and where?

Meetings take place on the last Wednesday of each month. If it clashes with a public holiday, such as Christmas, we simply skip that month.

We’ll start at 6:30pm and run until 7:30pm. Feel free to turn up earlier for some quiet working time. We'll round each meetup off with a show and tell to share what we've learned or accomplished.

We’re currently seeking a venue to host us but rest assured it will be in central Birmingham, near New Street Station, have free wifi and the opportunity to grab a drink and snack.

The next meetup is on Wednesday 26th October.

How to sign up

There is no need to sign up.

Everyone is welcome, just turn up on the evening.

We keep a little mailing list to let people know when the next meetup is. Feel free to add your email and we’ll pop you an email or two each month with information.
