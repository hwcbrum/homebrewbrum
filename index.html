Join like-minded people each month in Birmingham to exchange ideas, talk shop and get some feedback for that side project you've been working on.

What is Homebrew Website Club?

Homebrew Website Club meetups take place online and around the world every other Wednesday. Passionate people gather to work on their own websites and discuss the future of publishing on the web in the same structure as the classic Homebrew Computer Club meetings.

When and where?

Birmingham’s Homebrew Website Club meets monthly on the first Wednesday of each month from 6:30pm until 9:00pm.

How to sign up

There is no need to sign up.

Everyone is welcome, just turn up on the evening.

We keep a little mailing list to let people know when the next meetup is. Feel free to add your email and we’ll pop you an email or two each month with information.
